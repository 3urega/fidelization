# Business Rules

## Loyalty Points

- 1€ spent may generate points.
- Point calculation must be configurable per tenant.

## Stamp Campaigns

- A customer may have multiple active stamp campaigns.
- Stamp campaigns may be disabled without deleting data.

## QR System

- Every customer has a unique QR.
- QR codes identify customers globally.
- QR scans create auditable events.

## Subscription Plans

- Features are controlled through feature flags.
- Tenants cannot access features outside their plan.
